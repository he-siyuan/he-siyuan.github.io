About

Hey there! I'm Robert: an urbanist, a first-year BME student at the University of Texas at Austin, and a hobby photographer and cartographer. The bulk of my work is photographic: I love being able to take photos that capture a point in time, evoke a certain feeling, or frankly are just good to look at. I also like making maps, especially transit diagrams.

I have a lot of hobbies:

  • I like languages; I have the IPA chart taped to my wall.
  • I like birds. I'm trying to find better ways to take pictures of birds.
  • I like Wikipedia, as evidenced by my in-progress cladogram.
